## 🧠 About Me

I'm **Upender Naik Kelavath**, a passionate **AI/ML Engineer** with **4+ years** of experience crafting intelligent, production-ready systems — from cutting-edge **LLMs and GenAI pipelines** to robust **MLOps infrastructure** deployed at scale.

- 🤖 I specialise in **Large Language Models, RAG pipelines & Agentic AI** using LangChain, CrewAI & LangGraph
- 🚀 I architect end-to-end **ML solutions on AWS, Azure & GCP**, deployed with Docker & Kubernetes
- 📊 I transform complex data into **actionable insights** with Power BI, Tableau & advanced analytics
- 🧪 I swear by clean, testable code — **TDD, A/B Testing, MLflow & CI/CD** are my daily stack
- 💡 Constantly exploring the frontier of **NLP, Computer Vision & Cloud-native AI**
- 🌍 On a mission to build AI that makes a real-world impact
